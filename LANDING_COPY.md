## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free?**
A: Yes. No login, no credit card, no catch. We show you the audit instantly. We ask for your email after — never before — if you want the report saved.

**Q: How do you make money?**
A: SpendLens is built by Credex, which sells discounted AI credits to startups. If your audit shows significant savings, we may reach out about how Credex can help you capture even more. If your stack is already optimised, we'll tell you that honestly.

**Q: How accurate is the pricing data?**
A: We verify all prices against official vendor pages weekly. Every number in the audit engine traces to a source URL. See PRICING_DATA.md for the full citation list.

**Q: Is my data private?**
A: Your audit is stored locally in your browser. If you share a result URL, identifying details (email, company name) are stripped — only tool names and savings numbers are shown publicly.

**Q: What tools do you support?**
A: Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Claude, ChatGPT, Anthropic API, OpenAI API, Gemini, and Windsurf. More tools added based on demand — email us at hello@spendlens.app to request one.
